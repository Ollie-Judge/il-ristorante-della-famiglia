Ethics Our Story

We are proud to support local farmers and local independant growers in and around London, whether it be in an allotment or a multi acre farm, we will buy produce from you as we believe that even a local grower is an important corner stone of our community.

our grandparents moved from a small village in italy in 1902, to move to a London, a few years later they set up a restaurant which is still gooing strong to this day. since then my mum and dad took over from my grandparents and recently handed over the keys of the restaurant to me and my wife who currently run the business, we like to stick to our traditions and keep the business how it used to be when it first opened.
